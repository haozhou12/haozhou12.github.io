Welcome to my academic homepage. I am **Hao Zhou**, a Lecturer and Master's Supervisor at the School of Computer Science, Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications.

My research focuses on **AI security**, **privacy-preserving machine learning**, **federated learning**, **differential privacy**, and **verifiable federated learning**. I am particularly interested in building secure, private, and trustworthy learning systems for edge computing and large-model collaboration scenarios.

## Biography

I received my Ph.D. in Information Security from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2024. My work studies privacy, security, and trustworthiness issues in federated and distributed learning systems, including differential privacy, verifiable aggregation, robust learning under dynamic edge environments, and AI security.
