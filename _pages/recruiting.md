I welcome self-motivated students interested in AI security, privacy-preserving machine learning, federated learning, differential privacy, verifiable learning systems, and large language model security.

## Open Directions

- AI security and trustworthy machine learning.
- Privacy-preserving federated learning.
- Differential privacy for edge intelligence.
- Verifiable federated learning.
- Security of large language models and collaborative inference systems.

## Student Background

Students from computer science, cyberspace security, mathematics, communications, and related areas are welcome. A good fit usually includes strong self-motivation, willingness to read papers, solid programming ability, and long-term interest in research.

## Admissions

I supervise master's students in:

- Academic master's program: Cyberspace Security.
- Professional master's program: Computer Technology.
