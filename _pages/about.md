# Hao Zhou

I am a Lecturer and Master's Supervisor at the School of Computer Science, Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications. I received my Ph.D. in Information Security from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2024.

My research interests include AI security, privacy-preserving machine learning, federated learning, differential privacy, and verifiable federated learning, with a focus on secure and efficient learning systems for edge computing environments.

## Research Interests

- AI security and trustworthy machine learning
- Privacy-preserving federated learning
- Differential privacy for heterogeneous and edge learning
- Verifiable federated learning
- Security of large language models and collaborative inference systems
